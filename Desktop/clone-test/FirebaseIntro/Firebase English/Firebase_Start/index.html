Today I learned about Firebase 27/7/2016

Firebase is Google’s Product that makes App development easier. It provides a set of features that helps you develop, grow your audience and monetize your App. You do not need to worry about setting up an authentication mechanism to identify your users for example and it comes with a realtime database and an easy to access storage for more complex files. The hosting solution is free and very easy to set up.

why we need more women in tech and why I support Women Techmakers Hamburg 27/7/2016

When we watch the numbers, there has been a decline in the number of women entering STEM fields since the 1980s. In earlier times almost 40 percent of computer science graduates were women. Today the number is less than 18 percent.
We believe that there are several factors that have led to such a situation.
Media and education: Look at magazines targeted to women and girls, social media or movies as an example. Girls get messages all the time that technology is not for them. On the other hand boys get the message that it is necessary for a guy to be technically skilled.
A lack of role models and knowledge. A lot of people do not fully understand what is means to be a computer scientist and what people in those roles do all day.
This is what we want to change with our initiative Women Techmakers Hamburg. We want to provide opportunities to talk, experiment and network with people already in the field.
